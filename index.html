Figure 1

This is an interactive network plot of packages using the visnetwork package within the cranly plot method. The plot may take a couple of seconds to load.
Packages from the Bayesian task view are in blue and packages from Graphics are in green. You can drag and hover over nodes for more information and zoom in by scrolling.
